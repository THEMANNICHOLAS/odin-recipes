Blond Apple Brown Cookies

This is a blond apple brown cookie.

Blond apple brown cookies are the perfect desert for any person. It doesn't require much bake time, yet its absolutely delicious. Here are the ingredints:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup salted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup lightly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 6 McIntosh apples- peeled, cored, and diced

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) Lightly grease 9x13-inch baking dish
  2. Beat butter, white sugar, brown sugar, and eggs together in a bowl until smooth and creamy Beat flour into creamed butter mixtuer until well combines; add baking powder and beat. Stir cinnamon into batter. FOld apples into batter; spread into prepared baking dish.
  3. Bale in the prheated oven until center is slightly moist (about 45 minutes).
